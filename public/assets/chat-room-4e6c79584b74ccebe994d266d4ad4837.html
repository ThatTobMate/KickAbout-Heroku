Rules:

Welcome to KickAbout, a site where you can create your own football team and challenge others to a real match. Record your results and climb your way up the KickAbout League.

Challenge a team:

1. Create your own team or join a friends team.

2. When you have your team ready and you want to challenge a team to a match, go to the league table and click on the team.

3. Fill out the details and send off the challenge.

4. Go to the matches page and you will see the challenge you have sent off.

Accept or deny a challenge:

1. Go to the matches page

2. Here you will see any incoming challenges from other teams, you can either accept or deny the challenge.

Next

Submitting a result:

1. Once you have either accepted or had a challenge accepted, you will now see an option to update the result.

2. After the match has been played the winning team should enter in the final score.

3. Once the final score has been updated you will see your teams record has changed in the league table and on your teams profile.

4. The result and who you played will be recorded on the matches page for future reference

Points system:

Win: +3 points

Draw: +1 point

Loss: 0 points

Social:

Link your social network sites by clicking the 'Link accounts' button, for easier access to the site in future.
